This website includes several case studies of my recent work.

Google Bard I/O Preview

In early 2023 ChatGPT gained great public attention. To address market conditions Google leadership decided to accelerate efforts to release its large language model (LLM) to the public.

I served as a prototyper and designer on a five person design team to build a mobile prototype showcasing Google LLM technology in the Google App for Google I/O. I designed flows and implemented the flows into the prototype.

Throughout the process we had frequent reviews with senior design directors and product VP level leadership. Our prototype was shown to CEO Sundar Pichai, allowing the prototype to help shape strategy and direction for Google Bard.

Reddit Home and Post Details

I served as the primary designer and lead on new version of Reddit's logged out desktop and mobile web site. I worked with product and engineering VPs and EVPs for design reviews and feedback.

I partnered closely with design directors and the Head of Design. The project involved cross collaboration with numerous teams' PMs and designers (Ads, Search, Growth, etc.). I also mentored a more junior designer on how to structure and present work to executives.

The new design launched in 2023 and helped drive additional traffic and increased engagement on Reddit.com.

Google Ads Format Design System

Ads generate the bulk of Google's revenue, yet the ad formats used across Google are surprisingly balkanized. Many similar looking ads across Google differ in design and implementation significantly.

Serving in both design and engineering capacities on a team catering to several ads groups, I led an effort to streamline the use of ads across the search results page and across ads verticals (shopping, local, etc.). I helped establish consistent ad formats for designers and created shared components for engineers to use across ad engineering teams.

My work affected ads on Google's search results page, used by billions of people, and affected billions of dollars of revenue.

Google Ads + ML

I worked with machine learning engineers to design an interface to suggest novel ad categories to advertising agencies. The end product transformed a command line interface into a product usable by non-technical advertising agencies. This enabled advertises to use high grade machine learning capabilities to surface ideas that may otherwise be overlooked.

A quick prototyping cycled enabled the team to launch a preview product to our customers showcasing ML capabilities. This helped the product team determine requirements and understand the value of ML ad category suggestions.

The product received praised from our VP as "the most innovative Ads product in recent years."
