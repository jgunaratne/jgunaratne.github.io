EidoSearch is a FinTech startup where I served as the primary designer for the iOS app and a touch based FX trading desktop app.

Equity projections iOS app

The EidoSearch iOS mobile app allowed traders to explore equity performance projections. The app included lists of equities on the Dow Jones and S&P 500 with a chart projection exploration tool. I designed the app and implemented it as a native iOS app.

FX currency pair projections touch app

I designed and developed a touch-based desktop app that used a "tree map" data visualization tool to show overall market activity and permitted detailed explorations of currency pair projections.
