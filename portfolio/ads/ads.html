Google Ads

I joined Google Ads in 2017 where I primarily work on Google's sell-side ad products. My work in the group has included product design, prototyping and helping engineering with front-end production code on various ads products.

Machine learning based creative ad search assistant

Designed and wrote front-end for an app that uses machine learning on creative image assets to generate ad category recommendations. Worked closely with product management and UX design to iterate over UI.

Ad innovation showcase app

Designed and developed app to showcase new types of ad creatives that encourage customer engagement. Sold new interactive ad creative concepts to engineering by demonstrating value with an interactive prototype built in a highly compressed timeframe.

AdMob next gen prototype

Prototyped a next generation version of AdMob's flagship app. Worked closely with design and engineering to iterate over prototype used to sell novel ideas to engineering leadership and product management stakeholders.

Display and Video 360 app workflow improvements

Worked closely with UX research and design to prototype better streamlined workflows to improve customer tasks in the Display and Video 360 app. Added data visualization features and coordinated closely with UX research to meet a tight research deadline and iterated prototypes between user studies.

AdSense responsive design

Spearheaded use of prototyping on the AdSense team to help UX understand best use of responsive design for existing non-responsive AdSense desktop web products. Worked closely with UX to implement documented and undocumented responsive design patterns in an interactive form.
