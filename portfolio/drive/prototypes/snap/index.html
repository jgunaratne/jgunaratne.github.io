    Saving more lives

Our highly effective programs enable us to achieve a 91% live release rate. We are dedicated to making successful matches between our animals and adopters. With our high adoption rate and short stays for our animals, we have the ability to transfer in animals from other shelters that need relief and save even more lives in our entire region.
  

  

    
Dogs

    
Even though living in a dog rescue isn't ideal, most rescues (and some shelters) are assisting the dogs in more ways than just keeping it alive. Dogs can be socialized with other animals that help make them kinder and playful with all types of animals. Come in and take a look at our lovable dogs!

  

  

    
Cats

    
Did you know that most cats do not have a home due to no fault of their own? It is a common myth to think that all cats up for adoption in shelters and rescues are damaged. But, nothing could be less true! Cat rescues are full of lovable, active and healthy adoptable cats just waiting for someone to take them home.

  

  

    
Small animals

    
There are many small animals out there looking for homes, Rabbits, Birds or reptiles make great choices especially for families that have allergies to traditional pets. Come in and take a look at our variety of small mammals looking to find their forever home

An instant friend for life!

    
Ask anybody who has adopted a pet, and they'll swear their bond with their rescued pal is as deep as they come. No matter what circumstances brought them to the rescue, most cats and dogs for adoption are exceptionally affectionate and attentive, and make extremely loyal companions.

  

  

    
Join the pack and Support Paws

    
PAWS is a champion for animals, rehabilitating injured and orphaned wildlife, sheltering and adopting homeless cats and dogs, and educating people to make a better world for animals and people. We envision a world where all people recognize the intrinsic value of animals and make choice that demonstrate compassion and respect.
